Forest Hub

Welcome to Forest Hub where we inform people about the different kinds of forests in the world so that efforts to protect them can increase. If people are not informed then more and more forests will be cut down for properties or materials which will impact many animals living areas and also increase Global Warming since forests are huge in decreasing Carbon Dioxide.

We will talk about many forests such as the Amazon Rainforest, Congo Basin, Borneo Rainforest, Atlantic Forest, and forests in New Zealand.
